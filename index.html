My Portfolio

Welcome to the portfolio of Sofia Fabiano, an innovative interior designer and proud student of Arizona State University. With a passion for transforming spaces into functional and aesthetically pleasing environments, Sofia brings a unique blend of creativity, technical skill, and a keen eye for detail to every project. Explore a diverse range of designs that showcase the ability to blend modern trends with timeless elegance, creating personalized spaces that reflect the individual tastes and lifestyles of clients. From residential homes to commercial spaces, Sofia's work embodies a commitment to quality, sustainability, and client satisfaction. Discover the art of interior design reimagined through a professional journey that started at ASU and continues to evolve, inspiring and enriching lives one space at a time.
